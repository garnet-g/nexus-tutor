# NEXUS

## AI-Powered Learning Platform for African Students

Version: 1.0
Status: Product Vision & MVP PRD

---

# 1. Executive Summary

Nexus is an AI-powered personalized learning platform designed for students following CBC, KCSE, and eventually Cambridge curricula across Kenya and Africa.

Unlike traditional LMS platforms or content libraries, Nexus functions as a personal academic operating system that understands each student's strengths, weaknesses, goals, study habits, and academic history.

At the center of the platform is Nex, an AI teacher that provides personalized tutoring, practice, revision plans, exam preparation, and long-term academic guidance.

---

# 2. Vision

Every student deserves a personal teacher.

Nexus aims to become the AI-powered academic companion that guides a learner from primary school through university admission.

Instead of simply delivering content, Nexus continuously adapts learning pathways based on performance, learning behavior, and educational goals.

---

# 3. Mission

Help African students achieve better academic outcomes through personalized AI-driven learning.

Nex:

* Teaches concept

* Gives examples
* Verifies understanding

---

### Practice Mode

Student asks:

"Test me."

Nex:

* Generates questions
* Reviews answers
* Provides feedback
* Adapts difficulty

---

### Homework Mode

Student uploads a question.

Nex:

* Guides thinking
* Uses Socratic teaching
* Avoids immediately giving answers
* Encourages learning process

* Revision timetable
* Topic priorities
