1 What Is Nexus?

Nexus is an AI-powered personalized learning platform for students following the CBC and KCSE curricula in Kenya, with plans to expand across Africa.

Most learning apps give students videos, notes, and questions. Nexus gives them guidance, personalization, accountability, and measurable progress. It is designed to feel less like software and more like a personal teacher.

"Every student deserves a personal teacher."

At the center of the platform is Nex — an AI teacher that provides personalized tutoring, practice, revision plans, exam prep, and long-term academic guidance. Every other system exists to feed or serve Nex.

3 The Product — Six Pillars

Nexus is organized into six product pillars. Every pillar connects back to Nex and student learning outcomes.

1 — Learn

Structured, curriculum-aligned content: notes, explanations, worked examples, and short quizzes. Mathematics first; additional subjects in V2.

2 — Practice

Adaptive question engine. Topic quizzes with automatic difficulty adjustment. Targets weak areas and tracks mastery over time.

3 — Nex AI Teacher

The heart of the platform. Nex teaches, quizzes, guides homework using Socratic method, and creates personalized revision plans.

4 — Progress

Continuous academic tracking: subject mastery, topic mastery, XP, learning streaks, and study consistency — all in one view.

5 — Revision Plans

AI-generated daily study plans based on exam dates, current performance, weak areas, and available study time.

6 — Parent Corner

Dedicated parent dashboard: study time, academic growth, weak subjects, predicted grades, and weekly performance reports.

4 Nex — The AI Teacher

Nex is not a feature bolted onto a content app. It is the backbone of the entire product. Nex is warm, patient, Kenyan-aware (uses local examples like matatu fares and market prices), and adapts its language to the student's grade level. It operates in four modes:

ModeTriggerHow Nex Responds
Explain "Explain fractions" Teaches the concept, uses age-appropriate language and local examples, works through examples step by step, then checks understanding before moving on.
Practice "Test me on algebra" Generates questions one at a time, adapts difficulty based on performance (easier after 2 wrong, harder after 2 right), and summarizes weak areas at session end.
Homework Student pastes a problem Socratic method — guides thinking rather than giving the answer directly. Asks "What do you already know?" first. Full solution only after genuine student effort (3 attempts).
Revision "Exams in 14 days" Pulls mastery data and weak topics, then builds a structured study plan with daily tasks, topic priorities, and an exam countdown. Saved and tracked automatically.

Nex is powered by Gemini Flash (primary) with OpenAI as fallback. All teaching behavior follows the Nex Socratic Tutor Engine — no prompt is written ad hoc. V1 is text-only; Voice and Camera modes are V2.

5 Signature Features

Academic Health Score™

Every new student completes a diagnostic assessment. Nexus generates an overall Academic Health Score (0–100) with a subject and topic breakdown — showing exactly where they're strong and where they need work.

Example output:
Academic Health Score: 68/100  ·  Math: 52%  ·  ✓ Geometry (strong)  ·  ✗ Algebra, Fractions (weak)

Predicted Grade Engine

Nexus continuously estimates the student's likely exam performance based on mastery data and practice history. Predictions update dynamically as the student progresses. Visible on the dashboard for both free and premium users.

Knowledge Graph

Every curriculum topic is mapped to its prerequisites. If a student struggles with Linear Equations, Nexus identifies the underlying gaps (Fractions, Integers, Basic Algebra) and adjusts the learning path automatically — so they fix the root cause, not just the symptom.

Gamification

XP system, levels (Explorer → Scholar → Academic Warrior → Nexus Master), daily streaks, and achievement badges (e.g. Algebra Master, 7-Day Streak, First Diagnostic Complete). Leaderboards are V2.
